Moussaka

Description

Moussaka known as a Moroccan dish often cooked in large quantities. There are many variations on this classic but it often consists of layers of mince lamb, béchamel sauce and sheets of pasta.

This dish can be made to serve four or forty (if you have enough leftover lamb), and can be frozen once cooked making it perfect for batch cooking.
