Cottage Pie

Description

Cottage Pie is made with beef mince and potato topping. There are many variations on this classic but it often consists of beef mince, covered in mash potato and cheddar cheese.

This dish can be made to serve four or forty (if you have enough beef), and can be frozen once cooked making it perfect for batch cooking.
